Which account will send?

Gmail opens using whichever account is currently active in your browser — not necessarily the one you think.

To send from a specific account:
  1. 1 Open Gmail in a new tab
  2. 2 Click your profile picture (top-right)
  3. 3 Choose Switch account → select the account you want
  4. 4 Return here and click Open Gmail
Your emails & files stay in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
